Hey, I'm Marty

The indie developer behind DueDock

I built DueDock because I was tired of juggling multiple bill tracking apps that either required connecting to my bank accounts or were cluttered with features I didn't need. None of them had the specific features I wanted, so I decided to build a custom app that did exactly what I needed it to do ‚Äî simple, private, and focused on what matters.

Building DueDock has been a journey of learning app development and what it really means to create something that people will actually use every day. I'm constantly tweaking and improving it based on feedback from beta testers.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me in British Columbia, Canada, enjoying the mountains and probably thinking about ways to make DueDock even better.
